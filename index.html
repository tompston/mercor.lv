Building Materials

Work from the Riga port terminal warehouses. Constantly available specification for 5000-8000^m3 timber in multiple quality levels, which is purchased from all of the major sawmills in the Baltic States.

Specifications for the purchased materials are developed together with our partners on the basis of the market and customer needs, as well as the market situation.

Materials are sorted by Scandinavian quality standards.

Packing Materials

We offer pine / spruce packing timber in standard sizes of 14x70, 75x75, 16x75/100 and 19x75/100.

The packing materials are cross-cut to 800/1000/1200 milimeter lenghts, as well full-lenghts of 3 - 6 meters.

The materials are divided into multiple quality groups, in accordance to the customer needs.
